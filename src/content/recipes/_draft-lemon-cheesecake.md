instructions:
  - text: "Preheat oven to 160°C/140°C fan-forced. Grease a 6cm-deep, 20cm round springform pan. Process 250g plain sweet biscuits until finely chopped. Add 150g melted butter. Process until combined. Press mixture over base and side of prepared pan. Refrigerate for 15 minutes."
    timerMinutes: 15
  - text: "Meanwhile, beat 500g softened cream cheese, 180g caster sugar and 3 tsp finely grated lemon rind together until smooth. One at a time add 3 eggs, beating until combined. Add 60ml lemon juice. Beat for 1 minute. Pour mixture into prepared pan."
  - text: "Bake for 50 minutes to 1 hour or until filling is just set. Cool in oven with door slightly ajar. Refrigerate for 4 hours or until cold."
    timerMinutes: 50
  - text: "Dust with icing sugar and serve with double cream."
